Frequently Asked Questions



At LF Marketing, we're passionate about helping you create personalized merchandise that stands out and makes a lasting impression. Whether you're looking for custom apparel, promotional items, or unique gifts, our platform offers a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience. With our extensive product selection and exceptional customer service, we're dedicated to bringing your creative vision to life. Explore our wide range of customizable merchandise and let your imagination run wild. Get ready to showcase your style, promote your brand, or surprise your loved ones with one-of-a-kind creations. Start your journey into the world of custom merchandise today!

How do I place an order for custom merchandise?

To place an order for custom merchandise, follow these simple steps: To place an order for custom merchandise, simply browse our website and select the desired product category. Then, click on the "Add to Cart" button for the items you wish to order. Once you've added all your desired items to the cart, proceed to the checkout page. Fill in your contact details, including your email address and any specific customization requests in the "Additional Comments" section. Click "Submit Order." We will then reach out to you within 24 hours to further discuss your order and provide you with a quote.

Can I preview my design before placing an order?

Since our customization process is based on individual requests, we don't offer a real-time design preview. However, you can describe your design preferences in the "Additional Comments" section during checkout, and our team will work closely with you to ensure your custom merchandise meets your expectations. We will also send you a design proof before starting production.

What file formats do you accept for custom artwork?

We accept various file formats for custom artwork, including JPEG, PNG, PDF, and AI (Adobe Illustrator). You can email your artwork or design files to us after submitting your order. Please ensure that your files meet our requirements for optimal printing quality.

How long does it take to process and ship my order?

The processing time for custom merchandise orders depends on the specific product and the complexity of customization. Typically, it takes 3-7 business days for our team to process and prepare your order for shipping. After processing, the estimated shipping time will vary based on your location. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking details once your order is shipped.

Can I cancel or modify my order after it has been placed?

We understand that circumstances may change. If you need to cancel or modify your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but please note that order modifications or cancellations may not be possible if the production process has already begun.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure online payment options. Rest assured that all transactions are processed securely to protect your sensitive information.

What is your return and refund policy?

Please note that custom orders are non-returnable and non-refundable once they have been approved, and production has begun. These orders are specifically tailored to your unique requirements, making them ineligible for returns or refunds. We are committed to working closely with you to ensure your complete satisfaction with your custom merchandise.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale discounts?

Yes, we offer competitive pricing and discounts for bulk orders or wholesale purchases. If you're interested in placing a large order, please reach out to our sales team or submit a wholesale inquiry through our website. We'll be happy to provide you with a personalized quote and discuss any special requirements or customization options.

Contact Us

We hope this FAQ section provides you with helpful information. If you have any further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team. We're here to assist you throughout your shopping experience.
